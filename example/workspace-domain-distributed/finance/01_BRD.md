# Business Requirements Document (BRD) - Finance Domain
**Specification Boundary:** Finance
**Product Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Business Objectives & Goals
The Finance Domain governs financial ledger reconciliation, transaction records, tax calculations, and secure adjustments.

* **Objective 1:** Maintain ledger accuracy and full regulatory compliance.
* **Objective 2:** Enforce multi-factor verification for high-value financial actions.

---

## 2. Stakeholders & Roles
* **Chief Financial Officer:** Approves large ledger adjustments.
* **Accountant:** Performs daily transaction balancing.

---

## 3. Scope Boundaries
* **In-Scope:** Ledger book entries, reconciliations, and audit log mapping.
* **Out-of-Scope:** Warehousing details or shipping management (owned by Inventory Domain).
